Basexcel Technologies Limited is a world-class IT/Telecoms solution provider. Our area of specialization amongst others is to provide high quality world class and value-added IT/Telecoms solutions and services in the following core areas.

• IT/Telecoms Project Implementation
• Systems Integration and Support Services
• LAN/WAN Networking Solutions
• IT/Telecoms Education and Skill Training
• Consultancy Services
• Web Development
• Sales and Maintenance of Computers and Office Equipment

Management of Basexcel Technologies Limited has in place a minimum acceptable international standard of doing business and hence we excel in whatever we set out to do. The experience we have gained over the years gave us the foundation upon which Basexcel Technologies Limited was nurtured and is being developed.

OUR VISION

To be the best IT/Telecoms Solutions provider in Nigeria and the sub-Saharan Africa, providing top quality, world-class and value-added services at very moderate prices and profit and to be of immense benefit to staff in particular and mankind in general.

OUR MISSION

To use highly motivated technically skilled personnel, state of the art technology, pursue the satisfaction of the emerging IT and Telecoms needs of all men, organi zations and government agencies in Nigeria and the Sub-Saharan Africa. This we have resolved to dowith all sense of integrity and in a socially responsible manner.

OUR STRATEGIES FOR PRODUCT AND SERVICE DELIVERY

We have carefully designed a set of strategies that optimizes our resources and time, thereby eliminating waste and reduce customer waiting time while at the same time not compromising on our avowed culture of excellence. We achieve these by:

• Maintaining dedicated full time professionals.
• Maintaining our facilities and resources at their full service capabilities.
• Maintaining a competitive price.
• Training and retraining our personnel to ensure that our staffs are different
from others around us.
• Ensuring that we not only meet but possibly exceed every of our clients
expectations in all our engagements.
• Reducing exposure to systems problems by using proven hardware, solutions and
systems at all times.
• Cultivating a culture of continuous improvement in all our endeavours.
